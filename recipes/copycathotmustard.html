Copycat Hot Mustard

Back to Recipes

Description

This recipe provides a great alternative for the famous Hot Mustard from McDonalds

If you're one that prefers homemade foods, this is a recipe for you!

Ingredients

  • 6 tablespoons yellow mustard
  • 2 tablespoons dark corn syrup
  • 4 teasponns dry mustard
  • 1 teaspoon mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Combine yellow mustard, corn syrup, dry mustard, mayonnaise, vinegar, soy sauce, salt, sugar, and cayenne in a medium bowl and whisk until smooth and well blended. Refrigerate until ready to use.
