Grilled Cheese

Back to Recipes

Description

Grilled cheese sandwiches are a classic and a staple for simple eats.

Whether it's cooking for yourself, or multiplying the ingredients and cooking for many, a grilled cheese is sure to satisfy!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread of your choice
    • plain white sandwhich bread
    • sourdough
    • ciabatta
    • brioche
  • 2 slices of cheese of your choice
    • cheddar
    • gouda
    • mozzarella
    • pepper jack
  • 1 tablespoon butter
    • you can always use more for flavor and texture

Steps

  1. Butter the bread on one side and place the bread butter-side down on a hot skillet.
  2. Top with cheese, then place another slice of bread on top (butter-side up).
  3. Cook until the bottom slice is lightly browned, then flip.
  4. Continue cooking until the cheese is melted
