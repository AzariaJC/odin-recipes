Brownies

Back to Recipes

Description

Delicious brownies are some of the most lovable desserts in all of the south!

Sure to be a garden party hit, you would be best served by making these with love and care. That way, you get the most out of the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder

Frosting

  • 3 tablespoons of butter, softened
  • 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 table spoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.
  2. In a medium saucepan, melt the butter over low heat. Remove from heat and stir in sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Beat in cocoa, flour, baking powder, and salt. Spread batter into the prepared pan.
  4. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes in the preheated oven. Do not overcook.
  5. To make frosting: In a small bowl, mix together butter, cocoa powder, honey, and vanilla. Gradually stir in confectioners' sugar until smooth. Spread on cooled brownies before cutting into squares.
